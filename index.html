About Me

CSE @ IIT Gandhinagar

Hello! I'm Laxmidhar Panda, a Computer Science undergraduate at IIT Gandhinagar (Class of 2028). My fascination with Machine Learning began in a Probability and Statistics class with Professor Nipun Batra, and it has driven me to continuously learn and explore the field.

While I'm passionate about ML, I also love building engaging user experiences for the web, like this portfolio. This unique intersection of analytical and creative thinking led me to a role as a Design Core member for TEDxIITGN 2026, where I utilize my skills in graphic design and tools like Adobe Premiere Pro and Illustrator.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me playing badminton or unwinding with some music.
